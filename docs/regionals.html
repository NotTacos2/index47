Regionals
Regionals is the competition level between Qualifiers and the State Championship in many FTC regions. Although many regions have regionals and states merged Teams that advanced from qualifiers compete here for a spot at the State level. In Texas, for example, the state is divided into multiple regions and each holds its own Regional Championship. Not every state or country uses this level — some go directly from Qualifiers to States. The format is the same as qualifiers: qualification matches, judging, alliance selection, and elimination rounds, but the competition is significantly tougher.
